DOSA

A dosa / dosai / dosey is a thin batter-based dish (usually crispy) originating from South India, made from a fermented batter predominantly consisting of lentils and rice. Its main ingredients are rice and black gram, ground together in a fine, smooth batter with a dash of salt, then fermented.
Variations: Masala dosa, rava dosa, ghee roast ...
Main ingredients: Rice and black gram

vada pav

Spicy potato patties with crunchy lentils, served on a bun with spicy green chutney, red chutney

gulab-jamun

Gulab Jamun is high on the list of the most popular Indian desserts! (I mean, who doesn’t love donuts dunked in rose sugar coating? That sounds right up my alley.) You can find gulab jamun at festivals and celebrations—it’s a common food to serve during Diwali

pani puri

It consists of a round, hollow puri, fried crisp and filled with a mixture of flavored water ("pani"), tamarind chutney, chili, chaat masala, potato, onion and chickpeas. It is generally small enough to fit completely into one's mouth.
